The man who fought for freedom of INDIA

Mohandas K. Gandhi and other residents of Tolstoy Farm, South Africa, 1910

Here's a time line of Gandhi's life:

  • 1869 - Born in Porbandar, India
  • 1883 - Mohandes Gandhi Marries Kasturbai Makhanji in an Arranged Child Marriage
  • 1888 - Mohandes Gandhi Travels to London to Train as a Barrister
  • 1893 - Mohandes Gandhi Travels to South Africa to Work Under a Year-Long Contract with Dada Abdulla & Co., an Indian Firm
  • 1894 - Mohandes Gandhi Founds the Natal Indian Congress
  • 1906 - Gandhi Introduces Non-Violent Protest Philosophy of Satyagraha
  • 1915 - Gandhi Receives Hero's Welcome Upon Returning to India from South Africa
  • 1917 - Gandhi Establishes Sabarmati Ashram
  • 1929 - Indian National Congress Raises the Tricolor Flag of India
  • 1930 - Mahatma Gandhi Embarks on the Salt Satyagraha
  • 1942 - The All India Congress Committee Launches the Quit India Movement
  • 1944 - Kasturba Gandhi Dies at Age 74
  • 1946 - Direct Action Day, Also Known as the "Great Calcutta Killing"
  • 1947 - United Kingdom Passes the Indian Independence Act 1947
  • 1947 - The Partition of India
  • 1948 - Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi is Assassinated by Nathuram Godse
